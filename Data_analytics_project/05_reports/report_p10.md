# Practical 10: Visual EDA Report

## Objective
To perform visual exploratory data analysis using Python libraries Matplotlib and Seaborn.

---

## Charts Created

### 1. Histogram (Sales)
- Shows distribution of sales values.
- Observation: Sales are highly right-skewed, meaning most transactions are low value with few high-value orders.

### 2. Boxplot (Profit)
- Used to detect outliers in profit data.
- Observation: Several outliers exist, indicating extreme profit and loss cases.

### 3. Bar Chart (Sales by Category)
- Compares total sales across product categories.
- Observation: Technology has the highest sales contribution.
